Who Should Read This Privacy Notice?

This Papa Johns Customer Privacy Notice (“Privacy Notice”) applies to the sites and apps where it is posted, and any location or interaction where we collect personal data from you. This includes www.papajohns.com, our mobile sites and apps, all ordering channels, and your interactions with us offline, including information we obtain through phone or in-person orders. This Privacy Notice is between you and Papa Johns, its parents, subsidiaries, affiliates, and Franchisees (collectively, “Papa Johns”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). By interacting with Papa Johns online, in person, or by phone you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Notice.

Please note employees and job applicants should review the Employee Privacy Notice for applicable information related to their privacy.

What Is This Privacy Notice About?

Papa Johns values the privacy of its customers, and we believe you deserve transparency about how we collect, use, and share information about you. This Privacy Notice describes our information collection and use practices at Papa Johns, and informs you about your privacy choices.

Local Privacy Laws May Apply

This Privacy Notice provides a general overview of our data privacy policies. Papa Johns is required to comply with all applicable state and local privacy laws, which may vary depending on the state in which you live, and which may be more or less stringent than the policies described in this Privacy Notice. Where the state and local laws impose additional data privacy requirements than those described here, Papa Johns has adapted our privacy practices in those jurisdictions to satisfy our compliance obligations.

How Do We Secure Your Personal Data?

We use industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data. Our robust information security program includes measures such as access controls, network intrusion detection, routine training, firewalls, and anti-virus software.

While Papa Johns undertakes great efforts to protect your personal data, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee against unauthorized access, hacking, data loss, or data breach of our systems.

Information we maintain both within and outside of the United States. If you live outside of the United States, you understand and agree that we may transfer your information to the United States. This site is subject to U.S. laws, which may not afford the same level of protection as those in your country. Your use of our Web Site and Applications or provision of any information constitutes your consent to the transfer, processing, usage, sharing, and storage of your information in the U.S. and other jurisdictions as set forth in this Privacy Notice.

How Long Do We Keep Your Personal Data?

We generally keep your personal data for as long as we need your data to carry out the purposes that are described in this Privacy Notice. We may need to use your personal data after you stop purchasing items from us for compliance purposes, such as legal, tax, accounting, or other administrative issues.

When we no longer need your personal data for any legitimate business purpose, we will either delete it or anonymize it if permitted by law. Sometimes, your personal data is stored in backup archives or is otherwise stored in such a way that makes it technically unfeasible to delete it. In that case, then we will continue securely storing your personal data but will cease processing it and will delete it at a future available opportunity.

Do We Disclose Your Personal Data To Others, And For What Purposes?

Papa Johns relies on its internal staff as well as third-party service providers to manage daily organizational operations and pursue our business interests. Access to personal data is given on a need-to-know basis only, which means that we limit data access to only those who need your personal data to perform their duties or perform services on our behalf.

We may share your personal data with third parties in the following instances:

Franchisees and Affiliates We share information with our franchisees and affiliates to complete your orders and process your payments. We may also share information with our franchisees to provide local offers and promotions that might interest you.
Third party service providers We may share your personal data with certain service providers who need access to support our internal business processes. For example, we share information with vendors who provide mailing or email services, tax and accounting services, product fulfillment, delivery services, payments processing, data enhancement services, fraud prevention, maintaining and operating our web site and applications, data analytic services, and other business purposes. We may also share information with marketing partners who help facilitate online advertising. We may also engage vendors or service providers to collect information on our behalf.

When we enter into contracts with these third-party service providers, we typically require them to use your data only as instructed by Papa Johns, to respect and maintain the confidentiality of your data, and to have appropriate technical and organizational information security controls in place.
Promotional Partners We will share information with third parties who co-sponsor promotions. These partners may send you information about events and products by mail or email.
Mergers and acquisitions From time to time, Papa Johns and its Franchisees may undergo a corporate sale, merger, acquisition, reorganization, dissolution, or similar event. In these cases, personal data we collect from you may be transferred to an acquirer or successor of Papa Johns or its Franchisee, who may continue to use the data in the ways described in this Privacy Notice.
Legal Compliance There are other reasons we may disclose your personal data to third parties, such as under the following circumstances:
  • If we have a good faith belief that we are required by any applicable law, regulation, government authority, or other legal process;
  • If we need to do so to protect our rights or property, or if necessary to exercise, establish, or defend our legal rights, including to enforce our policies, Terms of Service, or any other agreements;
  • In connection with routine reporting to our affiliates
  • To respond to an emergency, or to prevent or protect from harm to you or others; or
  • Other business purposes, with your consent.
  • Data obtained through the short code program will not be shared with any third-parties for their marketing reasons/purposes.

  • The agent must be duly registered with the California Secretary of State

Authorized agents may submit a request by contacting us at privacy@papajohns.com..

Verification of Requests. For all Access Requests and Deletion Requests, we must verify your identity before we are able to process your request. We use the following two-step verification process:

Step One: You must confirm your control over the email address, phone number, and/or postal address you list in the request form.

Step Two: We attempt to match the data you provided in the request form to the data we maintain about you in our databases.

If verification is successful and we match your identity to a high or reasonable degree of certainty, we will process your request according to the timelines given above.
