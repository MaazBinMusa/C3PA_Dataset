1. Types of Personal Information

“Personal Information” means any information directly or indirectly relating to an identified or identifiable individual. Personal Information may not include de-identified or aggregate information, certain publicly available information, or information covered by other legal frameworks.

Category Examples

Contact information

Name, nickname, mailing address, email address, phone number.

This may also include the contact information of individuals that you share with us, such as when providing guarantors on a rental application within Apartments.com.

This may also include individuals whose contact information you provide to us if you would like to refer them to our Services. We typically send these individuals a one-time email to invite them to our Services. We store this information for the limited purpose of sending this one-time email and for tracking the success of our referral program.

Demographics

Gender, date of birth, age, language preference.

This may also include additional information that we collect through STR, such as household income, presence of children in household, educational history, and race and/or ethnicity, as permitted by local laws and regulations.

Business and employment information

Business contact information, department, job title / description, broker license number.

This may include your employment history within the context of a rental application on Apartments.com. This may also include your listing information, and broker performance within the context of certain CoStar Products.

Log-in credentials

Username and password, if you chose to create an account with us.

Personal images

We may collect pictures that you choose to provide to us or share in other public domains. Some CoStar Products may offer or require facial recognition as a means of authentication, which requires the collection of your biometric data. For more information about this, please see “Biometric and Geolocation Information.”

Social media information

Username, biography, and other information that you chose to publicly share on social media networks. We may also collect information associated with your social media account if you choose to use that account to log in to our Services.

Audio and visual information, including usage details

Log-on activity and browsing information such as search history, saved searches, and viewed properties. This may include partial or full video clips of your online interactions with us, including cursor movements, scrolling activity, and other items that you download or view.

This may also include audio and/or video recordings of your customer service interactions with us, meetings or calls that you host on Costar Products, or other calls you place to inquire about certain listings on the Services.

Technical details

IP address, geolocation information, information about your browser and operating system, unique device or hardware IDs.

Payment and financial information

This includes your credit card or other payment information.

For example, this may include information that you provide within the context of a rental application on Apartments.com and related brands, such as proof of income and income history. This may also include similar information within the context of a real estate financing and transaction, such as proof of funds information that we collect on Ten-X and related brands. This may also include your income or credit score within the context of our mortgage tools on Homes.com and related brands, and when qualifying buyers of businesses on our BizBuySell platform.

Identification

This includes Social Security number, other government issued identification (e.g., tax identification number), and vehicle information that you may provide within the context of a rental application on Apartments.com or a real estate financing and transaction.

Commercial information

This includes past properties, goods, or services purchased or leased either on our Services or in other contexts. We may collect this information through the use of third parties, including third-party tracking tools from data analytics providers and advertising and marketing networks.

Additionally, we may collect any other information that you choose to share with us on the Services. We may use this information and the other information identified in the above table to draw inferences about you regarding your preferences, characteristics, or behavior.

 

Biometric and Geolocation Information

Some areas of CoStar Products may require or request you to opt-in to CoStar collecting photos, facial geometry, and geolocation information from you when you log in to certain CoStar Products. We use geolocation information to provide you with relevant information that you have requested, such as local searches for rental properties and corresponding maps or directions. If you don’t want to use these location-based features, you can turn off or disable location services on your device or browser. We also use this information to authenticate you as the user of such CoStar Products, maintain a secure session, and detect potential abuse and fraudulent use of the CoStar Products. For example, CoStar may scan and analyze a photo you submit to us through the use of your device’s camera to confirm your identity for security purposes each time you access the CoStar Products. CoStar does this by using facial recognition technology to compare your photo to the most recent photo uploaded to your account. CoStar will securely store this information on servers located in the United States, and this information will only be accessed by authorized CoStar employees. We will retain biometric information until the initial purpose for collecting or obtaining such information has been satisfied or within three years, whichever occurs first. If you have questions regarding CoStar’s use of biometric or geolocation information, please Contact Us.

  • Directly from you when you provide it to us, or to others through the Services. This includes information you enter into web forms or chatbots, information you provide in phone calls, video conferences, subscription agreements, and contracts or other content you submit to the Services.

In addition to sources you may provide, this includes parties such as other property owners, tenants, landlords, agents, managers, or developers.
  • From third-party vendors including data analytics and email marketing service providers.
  • From researching public websites where permitted by the website Terms of Use. For example, this may include company websites, search engines, news websites, and social media platforms.
  • Automatically through tracking technologies (such as cookies, web beacons, or log files), including over time and across third-party websites or other online services.

For more information about how we use Personal Information collected through tracking technologies and the ways you may be able to manage it, see Use of Personal Information, our Cookie Policy, and Manage Your Personal Information.

  • Sending you prizes and gifts.
  • Short-term transient use, as defined in the California Consumer Privacy Act.

  • For any other purpose with your consent.

When you use the Services, we and third parties may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information, including Personal Information, to help us target content to you on the Services or elsewhere. Third parties use this information to provide interest-based advertising and retargeting on our behalf, which may include placing our advertisements on other websites you visit, including your social media accounts, or matching a cookie or device ID to your email address so we can send you promotional emails that may be of interest to you.

To make informed choices about targeted advertising, see Manage Your Personal Information.

Automated Decision-Making

It helps us to understand what sort of properties you are interested in and to categorize your activity to help us determine the Services that may be best suited to your needs.

6. Security of Personal Information

CoStar implements security safeguards to protect your Personal Information.

We take steps to secure Personal Information through administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to protect against the risk of accidental, unlawful or unauthorized destruction, loss, alteration, access, disclosure, or use. For example, we encrypt payment card information in accordance with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) and store sensitive information such as geolocation data on CoStar servers located in the United States with access limited to authorized CoStar employees.

Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted through the Internet, and where we have given you (or where you have chosen) a password, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential.

CoStar has designated a Cybersecurity Team to investigate and respond to all security issues and vulnerabilities regarding its offerings. You can contact this team at: CoStarSecurity1@costar.com.

7. Retention of Personal Information

CoStar will retain your information only for as long as is reasonably necessary for the purposes set out on this Global Privacy Notice, for as long as your account is active (i.e., for the lifetime of your CoStar customer account), or as needed to provide the Services to you. If you no longer want CoStar to use your information to provide the Services to you, you may close your account. CoStar will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, and as otherwise described in this Global Privacy Notice. Please note that closing an account may not make your email address, username, or property name (if any) available for reuse on a new account. We also retain log files for internal analysis purposes. These log files are retained for a brief period of time, except in cases where they are used to protect the safety and security of the Services, to maintain and improve functionality of the Services, or to comply with legal obligations.
