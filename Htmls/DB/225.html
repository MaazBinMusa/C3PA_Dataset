Privacy Policy

Information Collected from You

Manage Account

Some portions of the Lightcast website require you to create an account to access them. If you create an account, you will be asked to provide name and email address.

If you have created an account, you will be asked to provide your username and password when signing into your account.

We collect this information for the purpose of granting you access to the protected portions of the website and maintaining your account.

Contact Us

You are able to contact Lightcast for more information, to schedule a demo, or otherwise to submit a question or request. When you use the contact feature of our website, we will collect your first and last name, email, organization or company, area of interest, and the content of your message (which may contain additional personal information you wish to share). You also have the option of providing your telephone number.

We collect this information when you initiate contact with us for the purpose of responding to your email or other communications.

Subscribe to Alerts and Updates

You have the option of registering to receive alerts, updates, and future communications from Lightcast. If you subscribe to receive such communications, we will collect your email address.

We collect this information for the purpose of fulfilling your request and providing you with future communications from Lightcast.

Download Report

If you choose to download a report from the Lightcast website, we may collect your first and last name and email, along with your organization.

We collect this information for the purpose of providing you with the report and contacting you about your interest in Lightcast products and services.

Submit a Job Application

You are able to apply for jobs with Lightcast via the website. When you submit a job application, we will collect your resume, first and last name, email address, eligibility to work within the United States, salary expectation, address, current employment status, availability, location preference, and a description of your interest in Lightcast. You may also elect to provide your phone number, current employer, links to personal professional websites, references, referral information, and any additional comments and/or cover letter.

We collect this information for the purpose of evaluating your candidacy and contacting you regarding your application.

Information Collected via Web-Based Technology

Behavioral Information

Behavioral information is information about how you use our services, the areas of our site that you visit, what services you access (including what searches your conduct), and information about your computer hardware and software – including your IP address, geolocation, browser and operating system type, domain names, access times, number of visits, and referring website addresses. This information is necessary for analyzing the use of resources, troubleshooting problems, preventing fraud, and improving our services.

Cookies and Web Beacons

A “cookie” is a text file that websites send to a visitor’s computer or other Internet-connected device to uniquely identify the visitor’s browser or to store information or settings in the browser. Lightcast uses cookies and other similar technologies to administer our site and deliver a personalized experience for our users. Cookies enable us to serve secure pages to our users without asking them to sign in repeatedly. Lightcast also uses cookies to store non-personally identifying information about you such as past searches conducted by your computer. We do this in order to provide a better user experience (e.g., by remembering a user’s preferences when they return to the service). Lastly, Lightcast uses cookies to ensure the proper functioning and improve the efficiency of our sites. Most internet browsers enable you to erase cookies from your computer hard drive, block all cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please be aware, however, that some Lightcast features or services may not function properly without cookies.

Lightcast uses third party web analytics service providers to track activity on Lightcast's sites. The providers use cookies to collect information regarding our users’ visits to the sites (including IP addresses) and the resources they access on the Sites. The providers supply us with reports based on this information in order to help us understand how visitors engage with the sites, and to allow Lightcast to advertise our services to visitors when they navigate to other websites. Lightcast alone uses the information collected by the providers, and Lightcast does not allow any third-parties to use any of the information collected for their own purposes.

How Information Is Used

Respond to Your Requests

We use your personal information to respond to your requests, including communications and content downloads. If you ask us to do so, Lightcast may use personal information you provide us to contact you about Lightcast products and services.

Provide Services

When you set up a user account for one of our licensed software products, you may choose to give us personal information such as name and email address. We will use this data solely to provide our services to you.

Besides the information you provide to us when you set up your user account, our licensed software products collect only behavioral information. We use the information we gather for the purposes of providing our services, operating and improving our services, fostering a positive user experience, and delivering the products and services that we offer to jobseekers and educational institutions.

Enhance Website and User Experiences

We use the information regarding the use of our website to analyze and administer the site and track user movement for web analytics purposes. This website usage information enables us to provide you with an ever-improving site, service, and general offering.

Manage Account

If you create an account, we will use your personal information to administer the account. If necessary, we will also use your personal information to verify your identity and provide you with access to your account should you become locked out or forget your login and password.

Facilitate Products and Services

Career Coach

Our Career Coach product provides you with career and education information to aid in choosing a career or major course of study. We use the information gathered in the same manner as described above for our licensed software products (with the addition that we may use your contact details to respond to any queries that you may have).

Career Coach is a collaborative tool that brings or may bring together four separate parties to make the most of the tool. First, when you use the Career Coach tool, you are an End User of the tool and any of its associated customer services. Second, Lightcast is the company that produces the Career Coach tool and hosts it on secure web servers. When you are using the Career Coach tool, you are interacting with software that runs on Lightcast servers. Third, an organization, such as a university or private company (“the Organization”) has contracted with Lightcast for Lightcast to make Career Coach accessible in a customized format. As part Lightcast's contract with the Organization, Lightcast agrees to provide the Organization with aggregated, depersonalized demographic information gathered through the Career Coach tool. This allows the Organization to adapt to better meet the needs of its constituents. Lightcast has also agreed to provide the Organization with your personal information, when you – the End User – register on Career Coach. Fourth, third parties who provide services that are essential to delivering the Career Coach may receive information that is entered into Career Coach. These Third Parties include companies who provide internet transmission and hosting services. Lightcast may also from time-to-time make available aggregated, de-personalized demographic information, which is gathered through the Career Coach tool, to other third parties. Because of the anonymous nature of this information, it would be impossible for any third-party user of this data to relate it back to any End User to whom the data actually does relate.

Skills Match

Ligthcast's Skills Match tool operates very similarly to Career Coach. The relevant differences are that more Skills Match features require a login for access, and that Skills Match does not require you to provide your name when you create a login.

Accordingly, Lightcast is unable to comply with requests under this paragraph except where a requester has a Lightcast user account. Lightcast will review its ability to verify requests on an annual basis, and will update this Privacy Policy if Lightcast subsequently is capable of verifying the identity of individuals submitting a request without relying on an existing user account.

  • The right to request that we delete your personal information.

    • Lightcast's ability to comply with a request to delete is conditioned on being able to verify your identity to the level of certainty required under the CCPA.
