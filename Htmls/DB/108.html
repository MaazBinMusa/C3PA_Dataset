Buildertrend Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2023

At Buildertrend, your privacy and data security are our top priority. We strive to be transparent about who we are, as well as how and why we collect, store, use, and share information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or household (“Personal Information”). This policy explains your rights regarding your Personal Information and how to contact us or certain supervisory authorities in the event you have a complaint.

When we collect, use, and take responsibility for certain Personal Information about you, we are subject to various state laws in the United States, such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (“CCPA”), amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (“CPRA”), as well as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which applies across the European Union (including the United Kingdom).

By collecting Personal Information, we may be responsible as a “controller” or “processor” for the purposes of the GDPR, or as a “business” or “service provider” for purposes of the CCPA. Any reference to “Buildertrend”, “we”, “us”, “our”, or the “Company” is a reference to Buildertrend Solutions, Inc., its affiliates, and its subsidiaries, including, but not limited to, CoConstruct LLC, CBUSA LLC, Square Takeoff Inc., and ToolBelt Data. Please see the “Contacting Us” section below for our contact information.

We may also collect other information that you or others may provide to us, including the content that you or others submit, post to, or create within the Solution, such as photos, comments, reviews, searches, photo notes, discussions, project details and costs, and style preferences.

We may record, monitor, or otherwise collect details about your communications when you communicate with us (via email, phone, through the Solution, or otherwise) and when you use our software to communicate with others. If required by applicable law, we will provide you notice or obtain consent before recording or monitoring any of these calls.

2. How do we collect Personal Information?

Most of the Personal Information we collect comes directly from you, either through email, telephone, the buildertrend.com website, or the Solution. However, we may also collect information about you from other sources, including but not limited to third parties from whom we have purchased Personal Information. This helps us update, expand, and analyze our records, identify new customers, and create more tailored advertising to provide services that may be of interest to you. In particular, we collect Personal Information from the following sources:

  • Buildertrend customers who may supply information directly to us, including Personal Information regarding their own business, clients, contacts, or sub-contractors;
  • From publicly accessible sources, such as property or permit records, corporate websites, trade and business licenses, professional certifications, permits, trade association membership, and e-commerce marketplace profiles (from these sources, we may obtain business information on both business people and companies);
  • Directly from third party providers of business information, such as home marketing sources, social media profiles, or customer due diligence providers;
  • From a third party with your consent, such as a financial providers;
  • From cookies on our website (see our Cookie Policy for more information); and
  • From market research surveys and/or interviews conducted by our own employees or by third-party vendors.

4. Who do we share Personal Information with?

We may share your Personal Information with:

  • Our affiliates, subsidiaries, advisors, and consultants;
  • Our contracted service providers, who provide services such as IT and system administration and hosting, credit card processing, research and analytics, marketing, customer support, and data enrichment for the purposes and pursuant to the legal bases described above;
  • If you use our services as an Authorized User (as defined in the Buildertrend Terms and Conditions), with the affiliated customer responsible for your access to the services to the extent this is necessary for verifying accounts and activity, investigating suspicious activity, or enforcing our terms and policies;

We may also collect, process, and use non-personal, aggregated, or anonymized data in any way, including by disclosing, sharing, or otherwise making it available to third parties for various research, marketing, analytics, and measurement purposes. Additionally, Buildertrend may share such anonymous usage on an aggregate basis in the normal course of operating our business. This may include, but is not limited to, sharing information publicly to show the general use of our services.

Any Personal Information or other information you choose to submit in communities, forums, blogs, or chat rooms on our websites may be read, collected, and used by others who visit these forums.

We only allow our service providers and joint marketing partners to handle your Personal Information if we are satisfied that they take appropriate measures to protect your Personal Information. We also impose contractual obligations on service providers and joint marketing partners to ensure they can only use your personal information for the purposes outlined in our contracts.

5. International transfer of Personal Information

We collect, transfer, and store your personal information, both internally in our United States office and externally with our affiliates and third parties. These international transfers are necessary for a variety of reasons described in this Privacy Policy. Wherever your Personal Information is transferred, stored or processed by us, we will take reasonable steps to safeguard the privacy of your Personal Information. For example, before transferring your Personal Information from the European Economic Area (“EEA”) to the United States, we will ensure that we have an appropriate mechanism for the international transfer in place, such as a data protection agreement with standard contractual clauses which may be viewed here and, where applicable, are hereby incorporated as part of this Privacy Policy.

Your Personal Information may be processed outside your jurisdiction, and in countries that are not subject to an adequacy decision by the European Commission or your local legislature and/or regulator, and that may not provide for the same level of data protection as your jurisdiction. We ensure that the recipient of your Personal Information offers an adequate level of protection, for instance by entering into the appropriate back-to-back agreements.

6. Children

Our websites and services are not created for nor directed at children. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have information about your child or that your child has provided us with Personal Information without your consent, please email privacy@buildertrend.com.

7. How long do we keep your Personal Information?

We may retain your Personal Information for a period of time consistent with the original purpose of collection. We determine the appropriate retention period for Personal Information on the basis of the amount, nature, and sensitivity of your Personal Information processed, the potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure of your Personal Information, and whether we can achieve the purposes of the processing through other means, as well as on the basis of applicable legal requirements (such as applicable statutes of limitations). We will only retain your data for a period of time that is reasonably required for legal or business purposes.
