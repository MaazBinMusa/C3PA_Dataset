For California Residents: Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information

Welcome to our portal for submitting California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 (as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act of 2020, and its implementing regulations, collectively, "CCPA") requests to opt out of the sale and/or sharing of your personal information. This form provides a way for individuals (like you, or, where required by law, someone you authorize, also known as an ‘authorized agent‘) to request us to not sell or share your personal information to third parties.

For more information about your CCPA rights and our data practices relating to your personal information, please review our Privacy Policy.

We may share your personal information with third parties in ways that constitute "sales" under the CCPA. We also "share" your personal information when serving targeted advertising (referred to as 'cross-context behavioral advertising' under California law). You may request that we not "sell" or "share" your personal information on a going forward basis. Please keep in mind that if you exercise your right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information, you may still see ads on our publisher's sites, and some data collection will still take place (for example, to detect or prevent fraud or deliver the services you request from us).

If your request includes multiple email addresses, you are required to submit multiple form requests. If you wish to exercise more than one of your CCPA rights, please submit a data rights request through our Privacy Center.

When you submit this form, we will take reasonable steps to prevent your data to be sold to third parties, or shared for targeted advertising. When complete, someone from our Privacy team will provide confirmation that your request has been fulfilled.

The information you or your authorized agent provides through the form allows us to:

1. Understand the nature of our relationship to you;

2. Validate your legal rights; and

3. Confirm you’re allowed to make the request (in the case of an authorized agent).

*Note: please review our Privacy Policy for specific instructions on using an Authorized Agent.

Sovrn Publishers wishing to inquire about personal data rights should contact Sovrn Support. If you need assistance completing the form or submitting your request, please contact privacy@sovrn.com.
